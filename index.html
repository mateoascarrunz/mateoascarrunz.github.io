Hi, I'm Mateo! I have a great passion for web design and development. My background as an architect has given me a strong foundation and the necessary skills to evolve within this field. I bring all my ideas, creativity and enthusiasm to design and develop elegant and sophisticated websites.

For the past three years, I’ve been working as a graphic designer helping all kinds of companies with branding, logo, web design and creation of flyers, banners, and brochures. For the past 18 months, my focus has been on UI/UX design. I am always eager to learn new things. I discovered a passion for web programming, therefore I’ve also been programming in the last 8 months.

Heritage Me

Description

This was a prototype made in cooperation with the University of Torino. Where it has the function to show the architectural heritage of a city and have the option to interact with them through augmented reality. The idea is a vast database of the architectural heritage of each city which is accessible to the public through a navigation app in order to be able to explore them.
