Mateo Ascarrunz

Munich, Germany

Hi, I'm Mateo! I have a great passion for web design and development. My background as an architect has given me a strong foundation and the necessary skills to evolve within this field. I bring all my ideas, creativity and enthusiasm to design and develop elegant and sophisticated websites.

For the past three years, I’ve been working as a graphic designer helping all kinds of companies with branding, logo, web design and creation of flyers, banners, and brochures. For the past 18 months, my focus has been on UI/UX design. I am always eager to learn new things. I discovered a passion for web programming, therefore I’ve also been programming in the last 10 months.

Heritage Me

Description

This was a prototype project of mine conducted in cooperation with the University of Torino. This is about showing the architectural heritage of a city, where users have the option to explore and interact with them through augmented reality. The idea is to construct a vast database of the architectural heritage of cities, which are accessible to the public through a navigation app.
